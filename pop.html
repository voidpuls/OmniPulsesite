Privacy Policy for OmniSpark

Effective Date: January 12, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use OmniSpark.

1. Information We Collect

  • Command Logs: We log all commands issued by users for support and quality assurance purposes.
  • User Identification: We may collect information related to your Discord account (e.g., username, user ID) to provide our services effectively.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Enhance user experience and improve the bot's functionality.
  • Provide support and address user inquiries.

3. Data Retention

We will retain your command logs and any associated information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

4. User Rights

You have the right to request information about the data we hold about you. You may also request the deletion of your logs or information from our system by contacting us via our support Discord: Join OmniSpark Support.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via our support Discord or within the bot. Your continued use of the bot after any changes signifies your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Information

For any inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via our support Discord: Join OmniSpark Support.
